About Solvanta Consulting

We believe that business operations shouldn't be a source of daily trauma.

Solvanta Consulting is a small, practical office productivity lab dedicated to turning messy spreadsheets and chaotic reporting workflows into clear dashboards and useful outputs.

We are not an enterprise BI tool, a Tableau replacement, or a fake AI platform. We just build lightweight, polished, browser-based tools that make work slightly less annoying.
